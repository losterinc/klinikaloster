Professor Bartłomiej W. Loster is a specialist orthodontist with many years of
clinical and academic experience. He treats malocclusions in children,
adolescents and adults using modern orthodontic methods — fixed braces and
clear aligner systems.

He combines clinical practice with scientific and teaching work, ensuring that
every treatment plan is based on current knowledge and the individual needs of
each patient.
